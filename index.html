MAILZC App

Discover MAILZC, a self-custodial, privacy-first messaging app built on the Zcash shielded blockchain.

With MAILZC, your conversations stay truly private. Messages are encrypted and transmitted through shielded transactions, ensuring no one can see who you’re talking to or what you’re saying.

There are no servers, no accounts, and no intermediaries.

You stay in control of your keys and your data at all times.

Messages are stored immutably on the blockchain, making MAILZC censorship-resistant, decentralized, and always accessible.

Experience secure, anonymous communication that's simple, fast, and built for privacy from the ground up.
